Lists

Delphi-JEDI     This list is for discussion of topics, general and technical, relating to Project JEDI and the Borland Delphi products. It is NOT a technical support forum.

The exception to the "no tech support" rule is conversions of the C headers of APIs. This is a specialty unique to Project JEDI. You are welcome to post questions about the details of C-to-Pascal translations. Non-API tech support questions are regarded as off-topic.

Join the Delphi-JEDI list
JEDI-JCL     Public mailing list for general discussion of the Project JEDI Code Library (JCL). All topics related to the JCL, such as bug-reports, comments, suggestions etc., are welcome. Also used for announcements of minor releases.

Join the JEDI-JCL list

Join the JEDI-VCL list

Guidelines
Prepared by Matthias Thoma
  1. JEDI mailing lists and newsgroups are only for questions related to JEDI projects, not for general Delphi help. There are other newsgroups and lists for support questions. As a group of volunteers, if JEDI members will help out with problems related to JEDI software (including new API translations for the library) when time and know-how are available.

  2. JEDI does not necessarily endorse, support, or agree with the opinions or statements expressed in any of the mailing lists. Any information placed online is solely the view of those who posted it.

  3. These are JEDI forums. By participating, you grant to JEDI the unrestricted right to use, reproduce, modify, translate, display, perform, transmit and distribute any material you post to a mailing list or newsgroup in any present or future medium and for any purpose, including commercial purposes (as expressed in the Mozilla Public License.

  4. Copyright material must not be posted without the permission of the author.

  5. Material that is libelous, abusive, hateful, an invasion of anyone's privacy, harmful to other users, or harmful to the interests of Project JEDI, its sponsors or individuals in its community must not be posted.

  6. List subscribers and newsgroup posters are expected to show respect for others' views.

  7. Cross-posting of messages is not allowed. Please choose the most appropriate list or newsgroup and post it only to that forum. If your message is applicable to more than one forum, please post it to the general list (delphi-jedi@yahoogroups.com). Occasional forwarding of a single message from one forum to another is acceptable, as long as it is genuinely relevant to the other forum's activity.

  8. Do not add attachments - files, vcards, images - to your message. If you need to post files, post them to the Files area of one of the mail lists. Please note that you need a Yahoo ID to post files.

  9. Do not post HTML or other style messages. Post plain text messages only.

  10. Please follow the same etiquette as is described for subscribers to the Borland's newsgroups.
